## <i class="ri-scales-line icon-gradient"></i> Penalties and Legal Risks

Operating a website that collects user data (via cookies or trackers) without an explicit "Accept/Reject" mechanism puts you directly in the crosshairs of data protection authorities. In Europe, the GDPR and ePrivacy Directive are strictly enforced, and small businesses are not exempt from fines.

## <i class="ri-shield-check-line icon-gradient"></i> The "V2" Difference: Why Now?

Google recently upgraded to Consent Mode V2, adding two new parameters: `ad_user_data` and `ad_personalization`. These are specifically designed to meet the requirements of the Digital Markets Act (DMA). If your website doesn't send these specific "signals" to Google, your ability to perform remarketing (showing ads to people who previously visited your site) will be disabled entirely.
