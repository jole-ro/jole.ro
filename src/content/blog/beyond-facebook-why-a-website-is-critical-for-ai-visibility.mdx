## <i class="ri-robot-line icon-gradient"></i> AI Tools Don't Scroll Facebook

When someone asks an AI tool, "Where is the best local contractor near me?", the AI model doesn't log into Facebook to check your latest post. It crawls the open web, looking for **structured, indexed data**. If your only presence is behind a social media login, the AI simply moves on to your competitor who has a searchable website.
